Italian politicians tweets visualization

How much Italian politicians deal with important topics such as Europe, Work, Health, Migrations etc.?
Instructions

Select two politicians from the lists, and click on Go!. You will see the two selected politicians in the sides of the card below. In the middle you will see ... how much the selected politicians address some important topics, represented as pie-charts. The more the two politicians talk about a topic the bigger the related pie-chart is. The nearer a pie-chart is to a politician, the more he talks about that respect the other politician. Move and zoom the visualization, the pie-charts and the photos of the politicians to look at them better. Just click on a politician to see his Twitter timeline. Just click on a topic to see their most recent tweets in which they talk about that. Click Cancel to start again. Enjoy!
